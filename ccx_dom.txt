**
** Empty lines are ignored. No spaces after punctuation symbols.
** Padding with exactly 1 tab is mandatory - it defines hierarchy.
** 
** CalculiX keywords start with '*'.
** 
** Parameters start with '-'. ** Parameter values go after ':' and are separated by '|'.
** Usually parameter ends with '='. Parameters without '=' in the end are just keys.
** Plus '+' means parameters go together. '|' means parameters are mutually exclusive.
** No 'required' flag means that parameter is optional.
** If parameter starts with '\n' it means it'll be written with a new line.
**
