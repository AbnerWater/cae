*COMPLEX FREQUENCY

Keyword type: step

This procedure card is used to determine frequencies taking into accountCoriolis forces (cf. Section 6.9.3). It must bepreceded by a *FREQUENCY step in which the eigenvaluesand eigenmodes are calculated without Coriolis (do not forget to use theoption STORAGE=YES in the frequency step, ensuring that the eigenmodes andeigenvalues are stored in a .eig file). The frequency step does not have to bein the same input deck. There is one required parameter CORIOLIS.

Finally, the number ofeigenfrequencies requested should not exceed the corresponding number in thefrequency step.


First line:

  • *COMPLEX FREQUENCY
  • use the required parameter CORIOLIS
Second line:
  • Number of eigenfrequencies desired.

Example:

*COMPLEX FREQUENCY,CORIOLIS
10

requests the calculation of the 10 lowest eigenfrequencies and corresponding eigenmodes.


Example files: rotor.
